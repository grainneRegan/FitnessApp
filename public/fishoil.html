Fish Oils


About Fish Oils

  • Fish oils are one of the most popular supplements and for good reason. The WHO recommends 1-2 portions of fish a week as fish contains omega-3 which has been proven to have a multitude of health benefits. However, if you do not eat enough fish, fish oils can provide many of the same benefits. Omega-3 has definite overall health benefits, decrease risk-factors associated with heart-disease, reduce symptoms of inflammatory diseases, help maintain healthy skin and it has been shown that they can help slow age-related mental-decline.

  • In recent years there has been more interesting research which indicates that Omega-3 can help with weight loss and help with muscle growth. Fish oils have thought to improve muscle growth in older athletes by stimulating muscle protein synthesis. It is also shown that it can help muscle recovery, reducing muscle soreness and stiffness after exercise.

  • When choosing a suitable fish oil supplement, you should consider the amount of DHA and EPA which are contained in them. You should choose one with at least 0.3g of DHA and EPA combined per serving.
