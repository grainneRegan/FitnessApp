Roast Tofu, Chickpeas and Vegetables

Vegan beetroot, hummus and chickpea Sub

  • 225g extra-firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 250g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large bulb fennel

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
  2. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper
  3. Cut tofu into 2cm cubes. Spread out on kitchen paper and squeeze gently to remove excess water.
  4. Core the fennel and cut into wedges.
  5. Spread tofu and chickpeas along with the fennel out evenly on the baking tray.
  6. Roast in the oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Slice tomatoes into halves and cut the ends off the asparagus.
  8. Add tomatoes and asparagus to the baking tray and roast for a further 15 minutes.
  9. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

  • 300g cooked beetroot
  • 3 tbsp vegan pesto
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 large ciabatta rolls
  • 2 large handfuls mixed salad
  • 400g drained chickpeas

    1. Blend Â¾ of the chickpeas, the beetroot, 2 tbsp of pesto along with 1 tbsp olive oil and salt and salt and pepper to season in a food processor until you have a thick, hummus.
    2. Cut ciabattas in half and heat.
    3. Fry left over chickpeas in a splash of oil until crispy.
    4. In a separate bowl toss the salad leaves with the vinegar and remaining pesto.
    5. Assemble sandwiches with the hummus spread, salad and chickpeas and enjoy!
