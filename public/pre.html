About Pre-Workout

  • Pre-workout is used to increase the performance of your workout. Taking a pre-workout about 30 minutes before your training session gives you a burst of energy and increases focus allowing you to get the most out of workout. The main ingredients of pre-workout are often caffeine, amino acids, beta alanine and creatine. The high concentrate of caffeine (often up to 400mg, the equivalent of four cups of coffee) means it is not ideal for taking late in the day. However, there is also caffeine-free alternatives that are ideal for late-night sessions. Over time your body can adjust to the pre-workout and it becomes less effective. Therefore, it is important to not over-use pre-workouts so you can get the full benefit of this supplement.
