High Carb Meal Ideas


Protein pancakes

Ingredients
  • 1 large egg
  • 50g oats
  • 30g protein powder
  • 100ml skim milk
  • 1/4 tsp backing powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Nutrition
  • (Serves 1)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 418
  • Fat: 11.2g
  • Carbs: 39.3g
  • Protein: 38.6g

Instructions
  1. Add oats to blender and blend until fine.
  2. Add rest of ingredients to blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Spray a non-stick pan with low calorie cooking spray and place over a medium heat.
  4. Pour the mixture into the middle of the pan (makes about 6 small pancakes). Cook on one side for about 1 minute until golden brown then flip over and cook the other side.
  5. Serve the pancakes with your favourite fresh fruit and toppings!

Mug cake

Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp sweetener (sucralose/ stevia)
  • 2 tsp cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • ¼ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp skim milk (or plant alternative)

Nutrition
  • (Serves 1)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 190
  • Fat: 7.1g
  • Carbs: 24.1g
  • Protein: 6.2g

Instructions
  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a mug or microwave proof dish and whisk until there are no lumps.
  2. Add rest of ingredients and mix until smooth.
  3. Bake in microwave for 30 seconds.
  4. Cool for 3-5 minutes. The cake will continue to cook as it is cooling.

Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients
  • 1 large sweet potato (about 400g)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 212
  • Fat: 4.6g
  • Carbs: 40g
  • Protein: 3.1g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Slice up sweet potato into fries.
  3. Add sliced sweet potato, oil, salt, pepper and paprika to a large bowl and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  5. Spread out fries on tray and bake for 35 minutes.

Tortilla Pizza

Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp passata
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 50g grated mozzarella
  • 8 slices pepperoni
  • 8 slices jalapeño
  • 2 plain tortillas
  • 1 crushed garlic clove

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 226
  • Fat: 14g
  • Carbs: 23g
  • Protein: 11g

Instructions
  1. Heat oven to 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Combine olive oil, herbs, garlic and passata in a small bowl and season well.
  3. Put tortillas on a lined baking tray.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over the bases and sprinkle over the mozzarella.
  5. Add the jalapeños and pepperoni.
  6. Bake in the oven for 6-8 minutes or until tortilla edges are golden brown and the cheese is melted.

Vegan beetroot, hummus and chickpea Sub

Ingredients
  • 300g cooked beetroot
  • 3 tbsp vegan pesto
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 large ciabatta rolls
  • 2 large handfuls mixed salad
  • 400g drained chickpeas

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 639
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbs: 77g
  • Protein: 24g

Instructions
  1. Blend ¾ of the chickpeas, the beetroot, 2 tbsp of pesto along with 1 tbsp olive oil and salt and salt and pepper to season in a food processor until you have a thick, hummus.
  2. Cut ciabattas in half and heat.
  3. Fry left over chickpeas in a splash of oil until crispy.
  4. In a separate bowl toss the salad leaves with the vinegar and remaining pesto.
  5. Assemble sandwiches with the hummus spread, salad and chickpeas and enjoy!

Cauliflower Pizza

Ingredients
  • 60g tomato pasta sauce
  • 60g green pesto
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 100g mozzarella
  • Handful basil leaves
  • 1 cauliflower (about 700g)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 75g ground almonds
  • 30g parmesan
  • 2 eggs

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 884
  • Fat: 49g
  • Carbs: 29g
  • Protein: 39g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 140 degrees Celsius.
  2. Roughly chop cauliflower than put in a food processor until very finely chopped.
  3. Put finely chopped cauliflower into clean cloth and squeeze to remove any excess water.
  4. Spread cauliflower out on a lined baking tray and bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool and then return the cauliflower to the food processor along with the ground almonds, olive oil, parmesan, eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Blend until a smooth dough is formed.
  7. Turn oven up to 180 degrees Celsius.
  8. Spread dough over lined baking tray to form a circle, about 1 cm in thickness. Bake for about 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and spread tomato sauce and half the pesto over the pizza base. Top with the mozzarella, finely sliced.
  10. Roast at 250 degrees Celsius for 5-10 minutes.
  11. Toss tomatoes, cut into halves, along with the remaining pesto in a bowl and season with pepper.
  12. Top the pizza with the tomatoes and a handful of fresh basil and enjoy!

Vegan Chilli

Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 2 tsp smokes paprika
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 red pepper
  • 400g black beans
  • 400g kidney beans
  • 800g chopped tomatoes

Nutrition
  • (Serves 4)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 367
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbs: 48g
  • Protein: 12g

Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven 200 degrees Celsius.
  2. Peel and cut sweet potato into medium-sized chunks, evenly coat with 1 ½ tbsp oil, 1 tsp smoked paprika and 1 tsp ground cumin.
  3. Season with salt and pepper and roast for 25 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, chop the onion, carrots and celery and cook for 8-10 minutes with the remaining oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat. After, crush the garlic and add to the saucepan, cook for a further minute.
  5. Mix in the remaining dry spices and tomato puree into the vegetables and cook for one more minute.
  6. Add in the red pepper, chopped into small pieces, along with the chopped tomatoes and 200ml water. Bring to the boil then allow simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Add the beans and cook for another 10 minutes.
  8. Add in the roasted sweet potato and serve with guacamole, rice, coriander and lime.

Courgette lasagne

Ingredients
  • 500g lean minced beef
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 2 onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 150ml red wine
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 2 large carrots
  • 400ml beef stock
  • 550 ml low fat milk
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 large courgettes
  • 4 vine tomatoes
  • 20g parmesan
  • 40g vintage cheddar
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • ¼ tsp nutmeg

Nutrition
  • (Serves 6)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 336
  • Fat: 8.7g
  • Carbs: 28g
  • Protein: 20.3g

Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Fry mince over medium heat for 5 minutes, until golden brown. Finely chop the onions and celery.
  3. Fry half the onions and the celery over a medium heat for 5-6 minutes and season well. Crush the garlic, add to the onions and celery and fry for a further minute. Grate the carrots and add in, along with the red wine. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the tinned tomatoes, stock and bring to the boil. Cover with lid and turn down heat and let simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove lid and simmer for a further 15 minutes or until sauce has thickened.
  5. Meanwhile, put remaining onions in a separate pan along with the peppercorns and milk (reserving 3 tbsp) and cook over a low heat. Simmer for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and let cool for 10 minutes.
  6. After cooled, drain with a sieve.
  7. Put cornflour and reserved milk in a small bowl and whisk until a thick paste is formed. Then add to the strained milk and simmer over low heat for 5 minutes. Add the mustard and ground nutmeg and season with salt and pepper
  8. Cut courgette into thin slices lengthways. Blanch in hot water for 1 minute.
  9. Spread half the mince mixture out in a lasagne dish. Layer half the courgette slices on top.
  10. Repeat step 9.
  11. Pour white sauce over top. Slice tomatoes and place on top of white sauce. Grate the cheddar and parmesan cheese and sprinkle on top.
  12. Bake for 30 minutes.
  13. Cut into six slices and serve with a side salad.

Chia pudding

Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp sweetener of choice
  • 230 ml almond milk
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla essence

Nutrition
  • (Serves 1)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 241
  • Fat: 14.1g
  • Carbs: 21.3g
  • Protein: 8.4g

Instructions
  1. Combine all ingredients in a mason jar or small bowl.
  2. Fry mince over medium heat for 5 minutes, until golden brown. Finely chop the onions and celery.
  3. Let mixture sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to break up any large clumps of chia seeds.
  4. Cover mixture and refrigerate for 1-2 hours or leave overnight.
  5. Serve with your favourite toppings such as fresh fruit and granola.

Roast Tofu, Chickpeas and Vegetables

Ingredients
  • 225g extra-firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 250g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large bulb fennel

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 545
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbs: 59g
  • Protein: 30g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
  2. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper
  3. Cut tofu into 2cm cubes. Spread out on kitchen paper and squeeze gently to remove excess water.
  4. Core the fennel and cut into wedges.
  5. Spread tofu and chickpeas along with the fennel out evenly on the baking tray.
  6. Roast in the oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Slice tomatoes into halves and cut the ends off the asparagus.
  8. Add tomatoes and asparagus to the baking tray and roast for a further 15 minutes.
  9. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Banana Protein Shake

Ingredients
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 250 ml almond milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 140g non-fat Greek yoghurt
  • Ice, as needed

Nutrition
  • (Serves 1)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 362
  • Fat: 10.7g
  • Carbs: 32.6g
  • Protein: 38.2g

Instructions
  1. Add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth.
  2. If shake is too thick add more almond milk until the desired consistency is reached.

Vegan Stir Fry

Ingredients
  • 350g marinated seitan pieces
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 red pepper
  • 300g pak choi
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Rice or noodles, to serve
  • 200g black beans, rinsed and drained

    • For sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Chinese five-spice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • 1 red chopped chilli
  • 200g black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 175g dark brown sugar

Nutrition
  • (Serves 4)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 326
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 37g
  • Protein: 22g

Instructions
  1. Put all ingredients needed for the sauce, along with 50ml water into a food processor, season well with salt and pepper and blend until a smooth sauce.
  2. Heat sauce in a saucepan over a low heat for 5 minutes until thick.
  3. Toss seitan pieces with the cornflour in a large bowl.
  4. Heat a wok over a high heat with a little oil and stir-fry the seitan for 5 minutes until golden brown. Set seitan aside.
  5. Chop red pepper, pak choi and spring onions and stir fry in the wok along with the beans for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add back in the seitan as well as the sauce and bring to a boil for about a minute.
  7. Serve with a side of noodles or rice.

Southwestern rice

Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium green pepper
  • ½ teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 200g uncooked long grain rice
  • 280g sweet corn
  • 1 can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 400 ml vegetable stock

Nutrition
  • (Serves 8)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 198
  • Fat: 3g
  • Carbs: 37g
  • Protein: 7g

Instructions
  1. Chop the onion and pepper and crush the garlic. Fry onion and pepper and oil in a large skillet over a medium heat for 3 minutes. Add in garlic and fry for a further minute.
  2. Stir in rice, spices and vegetable stock and bring to a boil
  3. Turn down heat, cover and let simmer for about 15 minutes until rice is cooked through.
  4. Add in all remaining ingredients and cook on a low heat until everything is fully heated.
