Leg exercises


Goblet Squat

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, grip dumbbell in both hands, at top end; in slow and controlled manner, keeping back straight, sit down into squat until upper legs are parallel (or deeper than) with ground, slowly stand back up.

Box Squats

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, and barbell on shoulders (use rack at just below shoulder height, don’t lift off ground), OR dumbbells in hand, in front of box, facing out; in slow and controlled manner, squat down until bum hits the box, stand back up.

Hex Bar Squats

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, inside hex bar, laying on ground; squat down to grip hex bar handles, keeping back straight and lift using knees. Once standing, squat down to where upper legs are parallel with ground, then stand back up slowly.

Single Leg Goblet Squat/ Pistol Squat

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, grip dumbbell in both hands, at top end; in slow and controlled manner, keeping back straight, sit down into squat with one leg, while the other is kept straight and out in front of you, unused, until upper leg is parallel (or deeper than) with ground, slowly stand back up.

Barbell Hip Thrust

Instructions
    Sitting to the side of a bench, facing out, with elbows on bench, and barbell at your hips, grip barbell with hands and thrust upwards with hips to where your body is parallel with ground, and knees at 90-degree angle. Let down slowly.

Single Leg Romanian Dead Lift

Instructions
    Stand on one leg, with dumbbells in either hand, by your side; slowly let your upper body forward, letting dumbbells in hands hang, whilst keeping your back straight, and your non-standing leg back, until body is parallel with ground and arms are at 90-degree angle, slowly stand back up. Switch standing leg.

Nordic Hamstring Curls

Instructions
    Kneeling upright on ground, with partner/ weight keeping Achilles and feet anchored, slowly let yourself forward as far as you can before your hamstrings cannot stretch anymore, fall to ground, and push back up to kneeling upright.

Sled / Weight Push

Instructions
    Standing in front of sled, grip handles, keeping back straight, push sled along track using powerful strides.

Farmers Carry

Instructions
    Walk along with dumbbells at either side in either hand, using exaggerated strides, walking on balls of feet.

Box Jumps

Instructions
    Standing in front of box, feet shoulder width, jump up to box top, step down, and repeat.

Box Step Ups

Instructions
    Standing in front of box, feet shoulder width, step up to box top, step down, and repeat – optional: hold dumbbells in hands or other weights, to increase load.

Split Squats

Instructions
    Standing in front of box, facing out, on one leg and the other behind on the box; squat down on the standing, keeping chest out and back straight, and looking straight forward – optional: hold dumbbells in either hand to increase load.

Lunges

Instructions
    Walk along with dumbbells, plunging into a one-legged kneeling position on alternating legs, keeping the knee above the ground.

Reverse Lunges

Instructions
    Walk along with dumbbells, sticking leg backwards into a one-legged kneeling position on alternating legs, keeping the knee above the ground.

Copenhagen Side plank

Instructions
    Using a bench or chair, lie down perpendicular and stick your top leg above the bench and your lower leg under the bench, then elevate yourself to where you’re resting on your forearm and elbow (similar to the conventional ‘side-plank’),and your lower body is supported by the leg on the bench, keep your lower leg steady, and your core activated.
