Cauliflower Pizza

Bacon and Avocado Frittata

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 884

Nutrition
  • (Serves 4)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 467
  • Fat: 38g
  • Carbs: 7g
  • Protein: 22g

  • 60g tomato pasta sauce
  • 60g green pesto
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 100g mozzarella
  • Handful basil leaves
  • 1 cauliflower (about 700g)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 75g ground almonds
  • 30g parmesan
  • 2 eggs

  1. Preheat oven to 140 degrees Celsius.
  2. Roughly chop cauliflower than put in a food processor until very finely chopped.
  3. Put finely chopped cauliflower into clean cloth and squeeze to remove any excess water.
  4. Spread cauliflower out on a lined baking tray and bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool and then return the cauliflower to the food processor along with the ground almonds, olive oil, parmesan, eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Blend until a smooth dough is formed.
  7. Turn oven up to 180 degrees Celsius.
  8. Spread dough over lined baking tray to form a circle, about 1 cm in thickness. Bake for about 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and spread tomato sauce and half the pesto over the pizza base. Top with the mozzarella, finely sliced.
  10. Roast at 250 degrees Celsius for 5-10 minutes.
  11. Toss tomatoes, cut into halves, along with the remaining pesto in a bowl and season with pepper.
  12. Top the pizza with the tomatoes and a handful of fresh basil and enjoy!
