Cauliflower Pizza

Ingredients
  • 60g tomato pasta sauce
  • 60g green pesto
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 100g mozzarella
  • Handful basil leaves
  • 1 cauliflower (about 700g)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 75g ground almonds
  • 30g parmesan
  • 2 eggs

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 884
  • Fat: 49g
  • Carbs: 29g
  • Protein: 39g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 140 degrees Celsius.
  2. Roughly chop cauliflower than put in a food processor until very finely chopped.
  3. Put finely chopped cauliflower into clean cloth and squeeze to remove any excess water.
  4. Spread cauliflower out on a lined baking tray and bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool and then return the cauliflower to the food processor along with the ground almonds, olive oil, parmesan, eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Blend until a smooth dough is formed.
  7. Turn oven up to 180 degrees Celsius.
  8. Spread dough over lined baking tray to form a circle, about 1 cm in thickness. Bake for about 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and spread tomato sauce and half the pesto over the pizza base. Top with the mozzarella, finely sliced.
  10. Roast at 250 degrees Celsius for 5-10 minutes.
  11. Toss tomatoes, cut into halves, along with the remaining pesto in a bowl and season with pepper.
  12. Top the pizza with the tomatoes and a handful of fresh basil and enjoy!

Bacon and Avocado Frittata

Ingredients
  • 8 bacon medallions
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 large avocado
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 small red chilli
  • 200g mixed salad leaves
  • 12 baby plum tomatoes

Nutrition
  • (Serves 4)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 467
  • Fat: 38g
  • Carbs: 7g
  • Protein: 22g

Instructions
  1. Fry rashers on high heat until crispy. Roughly chop and set aside.
  2. Warm 1 tbsp oil in the pan. Beat the eggs and then mix in the rashers and season with salt and pepper. Pour the mixture into the pan.
  3. Cook on a low heat for 8 minutes until almost set.
  4. Add the avocado, cut into slices.
  5. Grill for 4 minutes.
  6. Mix the remaining oil, finely chopped chilli, vinegar and mustard in a large bowl. Toss in the salad leaves and the tomatoes cut into halves.
  7. Cut the frittata into wedges and serve along with the salad.

Loaded Cauliflower

Ingredients
  • 500g cauliflower
  • 100g sour cream
  • 2 rashers
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 80g cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp chives

Nutrition
  • (Serves 4)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 289
  • Fat: 24.1g
  • Carbs: 7.1g
  • Protein: 11.4g

Instructions
  1. Fry rashers on high heat until crispy. Roughly chop and set aside.
  2. Chop cauliflower and add to a microwave safe bowl with 2 tbsp water.
  3. Cover with cling film and microwave for 5-8 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Drain excess water and let cool for a few minutes.
  5. Add cauliflower to a food processor and blend until fluffy.
  6. Add butter, sour cream and garlic powder and blend until smooth.
  7. Add cauliflower mixture to bowl and combine with the chives and half of the cheese and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Top the loaded cauliflower with the remaining cheese and the rashers and microwave for a couple minutes until the cheese is melted.

Keto Brownie

Ingredients
  • 170g butter
  • 85g keto chocolate chips
  • 60g almond flour
  • 25g cocoa powder
  • 3 large eggs
  • 150g granulated sweetener of choice
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder

Nutrition
  • (Serves 12)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 180
  • Fat: 17g
  • Carbs: 5g
  • Protein: 3g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  2. Line 8x8 inch pan with greaseproof paper.
  3. Combine all dry ingredients together in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Melt butter and half the chocolate over a medium heat until fully melted.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs until frothy.
  6. Gradually add in the chocolate mixture to the eggs, continuously stirring until fully combined.
  7. Mix in the dry ingredients a little at a time. Fold the remaining chocolate chips into the brownie batter.
  8. Transfer into the pan.
  9. Bake for about 15 minutes until cooked through.
  10. Allow to cool for 20 minutes before cutting into 12 brownies.

Keto Carrot Cake Bars

Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 125 ml milk of choice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 50g granulated sweetener of choice
  • 120g almond butter
  • 60ml sugar free syrup
  • 2 tbsp grated carrots
  • 60 ml pumpkin puree
  • 110g coconut flour

    • For icing:


  • 250g cream cheese
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp granulated sweetener of choice

Nutrition
  • (Serves 9)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 235
  • Fat: 12g
  • Carbs: 12g
  • Protein: 10g

Instructions
  1. Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with greaseproof paper.
  2. Combine the flour, protein powder, salt, sweetener and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Mix in the grated carrots and pumpkin puree.
  4. Melt almond butter and syrup over a medium heat until melted. Whisk in the vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into dry ingredients, along with the milk and mix until fully combined.
  6. Transfer into baking pan and spread out evenly.
  7. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  8. Combine all ingredients for icing in a mixing bowl and mix until smooth.
  9. Take bars out of fridge and spread icing over the top.
  10. Place bake in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.
  11. Once set cut into 9, evenly sized pieces.

Roast Tofu, Chickpeas and Vegetables

Ingredients
  • 225g extra-firm tofu
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 20 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 bunch asparagus
  • 250g canned chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large bulb fennel

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 545
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbs: 59g
  • Protein: 30g

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
  2. Line a baking tray with greaseproof paper
  3. Cut tofu into 2cm cubes. Spread out on kitchen paper and squeeze gently to remove excess water.
  4. Core the fennel and cut into wedges.
  5. Spread tofu and chickpeas along with the fennel out evenly on the baking tray.
  6. Roast in the oven for 25 minutes.
  7. Slice tomatoes into halves and cut the ends off the asparagus.
  8. Add tomatoes and asparagus to the baking tray and roast for a further 15 minutes.
  9. 10) Let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Keto Fishcakes

Ingredients
  • 450g cod
  • 35g coconut flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • Handful parsley, chopped
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Nutrition
  • (Serves 6)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 153
  • Fat: 7g
  • Carbs: 5g
  • Protein: 16g

Instructions
  1. Season cod with salt and pepper.
  2. Fry fish with a little oil, over a medium heat for 10 minutes.
  3. After cooked, use a fork to break cod up into small pieces.
  4. Add in the parsley, onions, flour and eggs along with the spices.
  5. Coarsely blend mixture in a food processor.
  6. Compress mixture into fishcakes.
  7. With the remaining oil, fry fish cakes over a medium heat for about 3 minutes on each side.
  8. Serve with a side salad.

Vegan beetroot, hummus and chickpea Sub

Ingredients
  • 300g cooked beetroot
  • 3 tbsp vegan pesto
  • Olive oil
  • 2 tsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 large ciabatta rolls
  • 2 large handfuls mixed salad
  • 400g drained chickpeas

Nutrition
  • (Serves 2)
  • Per serving:
  • Calories: 639
  • Fat: 22g
  • Carbs: 77g
  • Protein: 24g

Instructions
  1. Blend ¾ of the chickpeas, the beetroot, 2 tbsp of pesto along with 1 tbsp olive oil and salt and salt and pepper to season in a food processor until you have a thick, hummus.
  2. Cut ciabattas in half and heat.
  3. Fry left over chickpeas in a splash of oil until crispy.
  4. In a separate bowl toss the salad leaves with the vinegar and remaining pesto.
  5. Assemble sandwiches with the hummus spread, salad and chickpeas and enjoy!
