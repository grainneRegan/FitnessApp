Maltodextrin


About L-Glutamine

  • Maltodextrin is a supplement made from potato starch, wheat or corn. The main benefit of Maltodextrin is that it is a great source of quick energy. It can be taken before or after an intense training session. It is ideal for increasing endurance for a long workout, to give a quick boost of energy for when you start to lag or can be used to help recovery, in order to restore glycogen stores. Maltrodextrin has a very high glycaemic index and as a result, could raise your blood sugar. It has also been suggested that it can affect gut health. Overall, it is safe to consume in small quantities, but if any issues arise when taking this supplement, you should consult your GP.
