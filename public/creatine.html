About Creatine

  • Creatine is one of the most popular supplements used in training and can improve energy during training and enhance muscle synthesis. It is natural substance that is produced in the body already in low quantities and can be found in protein rich foods including fish and meat. Creatine can help make adenosine triphosphate (ATP), which provides energy to the muscles, allowing you to train at a higher intensity. It also promotes an increase in muscle size and strength, it pulls water into the cells of the muscles, causing an increase in size of the muscles. Taking creatine will cause an increase in bodyweight, normally 2-4 pounds within a week or two, this weight comes from the water retention that gives your muscles a fuller appearance. It also causes an increase in the level of the hormone IGF-1, which triggers the formation of new protein and as a result, increases muscle mass and strength. About 5g of creatine per day is ideal for improvement in strength and performance capabilities.
