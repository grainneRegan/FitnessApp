Back, Chest & Shoulder Exercises


Dumbbell Row

Instructions
    Kneel on bench with one arm and same sided knee, with other leg standing rigid, and dumbbell in free hand; let the dumbbell hang, then slowly bring dumbbell up to chest, and slowly let down to hang again.

Lateral Pulldown

Instructions
    Sat on box or bench, under Cable Pulley Machine (or pull-up bar, if using a strong resistance band), use a wide grip, where arms are making a 90 degrees angle, and pull down to where bar is at eye level, or to where you can not pull the resistance band anymore, in a controlled manner.

Resistance Band Pull Apart

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width, holding resistance band out in front; pull resistance band apart in a slow and controlled manner.

Renegade Dumbbell row

Instructions
    Get into push up position, whilst gripping dumbbells in both hands; using alternative hands, bring dumbbell up to hip and let back down to rest.

Dumbbell Lying Flyes

Instructions
    Lie on bench with dumbbells in both hands, with arms straight, pointing upwards; slowly let down arms, out wide, as to where you are making a ‘T’ with upper body at peak. Slowly bring back up to peak.

YTW

Instructions
    Lie on bench face down, with dumbbells in either hand, hanging down; in a controlled manner, make a ‘Y’ with your upper body, raising arms at a 45 degree angle, then proceed to make a ‘T’ with your upper body, raising arms straight out, and then proceed to bring your arms into a 90 degree angle at the elbow, and raise up to make a ‘W’ with your arms and head, then bring to rest.

Band Resisted Flye

Instructions
    Stand in front of cable machine or poll with resistance tied to it, with cable or resistance band in hand(s) , in a ‘T’ pose; in a slow and controlled manner, move your arms forward to where they meet in front of you, then slowly let back to spread wide, ‘T’ pose.

Incline Dumbell Flye

Instructions
    Lie on bench, elevated to 30 degrees or so, with dumbbells in both hands, with arms straight, pointing upwards; slowly let down arms, out wide, as to where you are making a ‘T’ with upper body at peak. Slowly bring back up to peak.

Prone Flye

Instructions
    Hold a dumbbell in each hand and get into pushup position on the floor with palms facing each other. Spread your arms apart as in a normal dumbbell flye and lower your body until you feel a stretch in your chest, then squeeze the dumbbells and bring your hands back to pushup position. Keep your abs and glutes braced and your back flat throughout. If using plate-loaded dumbbells, you may be able to roll the weight plates on the floor during the flye. Otherwise, you can place a towel under each dumbbell to facilitate sliding. If this is too difficult, perform the exercise on your knees.

Barbell Bench Press

Instructions
    Grasp the bar just outside shoulder-width and arch your back so there’s space between your lower back and the bench. Pull the bar out of the rack and lower it to your sternum, tucking your elbows about 45° to your sides. When the bar touches your body, drive your feet hard into the floor and press the bar back up.
