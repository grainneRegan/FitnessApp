Arm exercises


Dumbbell Bicep Curls

Instructions
    Sitting or standing, grip two dumbbells in either hand, at your side; in a controlled fashion, using alternating hands, twist and raise one dumbbell to your chest, then let it down and raise opposite dumbbell etc.

Tricep Dips

Instructions
    Sitting to the side of a bench, facing out, raise yourself up by putting arms behind you, on the bench and lift yourself up to where your arms are straight; slowly lower yourself down to where your bum touches the ground, or where your arms wont bend anymore, slowly straighten arms to raise yourself up again.

Seated Overhead Tricep Extensions

Instructions
    Sit on bench with one dumbbell in both hands, with arms behind head, dumbbell behind head; slowly straighten arms to where dumbbell is above head, slowly lower back down behind your head.

Lying Dumbell Tricep Extensions

Instructions
    Lie on bench with dumbbells in both hands, with hands and dumbbells beside/under the bench; slowly straighten arms to where dumbbells are in front of you, above your head.

Tricep Rope Pulldown

Instructions
    Stand in front of Cable Pulley Machine, with feet shoulder width and core tightened, grip rope; pull down slowly until your arms straighten, let back up slowly.

Close-grip Barbell Curls

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, grip barbell narrowly with both hands, in front of hips; slowly bring barbell up to chest, using just your arms, then let barbell back down to hips.

Chin Ups

Instructions
    Grip pull up bar with palms facing out, pull up to where your chin is level with or above bar, let down slowly.

Narrow Grip Pulls up

Instructions
    Grip pull up bar narrowly with palms facing in, pull up to where your chin is level with or above bar, let down slowly.

Push ups

Instructions
    Get into push up position; let yourself slowly down to where your chest is touching the ground, then push back up to where your arms are straight again – optional: can raise feet off ground (onto bench or box) if too easy, can rest on knees (rather than full length) if too difficult.

Resistance Band Face Pull

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, in front of resistance band anchored at eye level to pole or bar; grip resistance band in both arms, pull to where hands are either side of face, making a triangle with band.

Dumbbell Bench Press

Instructions
    Lie on bench with dumbbells in both hands, with hands and dumbbells beside head, facing upwards; in slow and controlled manner, push dumbbells forward and upward to where they meet in front of your face, let back down slowly.

Dumbbell Lateral Raises

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width and core tightened, grip dumbbells at either side, rise both in a controlled manner until making a ‘T’ with upper body, let down slowly.
