Core exercises


Plank

Instructions
    face down, elevated on your elbows and toes, keeping core tightened (activated) and bum down (not in a peak) – optional: can use weighted plates on back to increase load.

Plank Shoulder tap

Instructions
    press up position, keeping slow and controlled, raise one hand to tap opposite shoulder, bring down and raise opposite hand to shoulder opposite to it etc.

Side Plank (long-lever)

Instructions
    elevated on one elbow and forearm down to your foot/ ankle, keeping core tightened (activated) - optional: leg raises (raise top leg in slow, controlled way), with weighted fly (top arm goes under and back up, holding (optional) weight)

Side Plank (short-lever)

Instructions
    elevated on one elbow and forearm down to your knee, keeping core tightened (activated) - optional: leg raises (raise top leg in slow, controlled way), with weighted fly (top arm goes under and back up, holding (optional) weight)

Panther Shoulder Tap

Instructions
    similar plank shoulder tap, get into kneeling position, then raise knees off the floor, keep body steady, raise hand to opposite shoulder etc.

Sit up

Instructions
    lie on back with knees up in peaks and arms (ideally) behind head, raise upper body, without using arms, to meet knees and let back to lying flat and knees up – optional: can integrate twists into sit ups, where, once at knees, can bring elbow to touch opposite knee, back down and opposite elbow to its opposite knee, once back up.

Instructions

Row Your Boat

Instructions
    sit with bum only on floor, legs elevated by an inch; as if moving a load across your body (if not using exercise ball), from your left to right or vice versa, move your upper body to ‘move’ this load and then set it down on other side of you, while keeping legs slightly elevated - optional: with exercise ball, start small so form does not suffer.


Leg Raises

Instructions
    lie on back with legs raised above floor by an inch, tighten core; in slow, controlled fashion raise both legs to where they are directly upright, let down to an inch above ground again etc.
